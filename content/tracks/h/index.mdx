## What & why

The difference between a demo and a product: observability, guardrails against injection,
cost/latency discipline, versioning, least-privilege permissions, and a written record of
your architecture decisions.

## What you must cover

- Prompt/agent versioning
- ADR discipline — one per major architecture choice
- Retrieval access control (row/user-level)
- PII/output filtering + leakage test
- Agent permission boundaries (least privilege)
- Supply-chain test: poisoned doc/tool injection
